# Withdrawals & Account Safety

> Note: withdrawal thresholds and requirements may change depending on regional compliance and risk controls. The latest in-app display prevails.

## Where is the withdraw button?

My Dashboard -> **Withdraw**

## What steps might be required?

- basic account verification (TBD),
- Proof-of-Human or risk review for suspicious accounts,
- confirming a receiving address.

## Why can withdrawals be delayed?

- chain congestion,
- risk review,
- campaign settlement window not finished.

## Security tips

- never share verification codes, private keys, or seed phrases,
- beware of phishing links and fake support,
- only use official website/app entry points.
