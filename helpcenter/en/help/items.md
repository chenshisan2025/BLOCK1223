# Item Packs

Item packs enhance gameplay in both Story and Endless modes.

## Where can I use items?

- Story Mode: allowed
- Endless Mode: allowed

## Usage caps

- Story Mode: typically unlimited (as shown in-app)
- Endless Mode: each item type up to **5 uses per run**

## Item pack revenue split

- **50%** -> **Burn**
- **40%** -> **Treasury**
- **10%** -> **Referral**
