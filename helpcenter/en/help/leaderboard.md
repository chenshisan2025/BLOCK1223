# Leaderboard & Endless Tickets

Endless Mode is designed for competition. Ticket spend contributes to the leaderboard prize pool and is verifiable on-chain.

## Ticket rules

- First Endless entry is **free daily**.
- Additional entries cost **20 BWT** per ticket.

## Limits (per run)

- Up to **5 revives**
- Each item type up to **5 uses**

## Ticket split (20 BWT)

- **50%** -> **Leaderboard Pool**
- **30%** -> **Burn**
- **10%** -> **Referral**
- **10%** -> **Treasury**

## Prize distribution

Exact payout rules (Top N, tiers, ratios) depend on the current leaderboard configuration shown on the leaderboard page.

> Reminder: leaderboard rewards are subject to eligibility and risk controls.
