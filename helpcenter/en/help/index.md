## Key Terms

- **BWT**: the BLOCKWORLD Token used for in-game sinks (revives, tickets, item packs) and reward settlement.
- **Mystery Box**: an in-game random box that may contain BWT, sponsor rewards, or event drops.
- **Treasure Box**: an entry you receive when you revive using BWT. Draws happen every 2 hours.
- **Transparency Dashboard**: a real-time / near-real-time view of on-chain flows (pools, burn, referral, treasury) plus a Tx feed you can verify.
